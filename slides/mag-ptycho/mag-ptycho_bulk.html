  • Consider bulk antiferromagnetic NiO
    • Ni↑Ni_{\uparrow}Ni↑​ (pink) spins up and Ni↓Ni_{\downarrow}Ni↓​ (green) align on alternating [111] planes
    • Magnetic contribution ~500x smaller than electrostatic contribution
  • Electrostatic contribution → invariant under time-reversal symmetry
  • Magnetic contribution → changes sign under time-reversal symmetry
    • Inversion-symmetry: "flip" sample and record two measurements:
ϕe=12(ϕ→+ϕ←)andϕm=12(ϕ→−ϕ←) \phi_e = \frac{1}{2}\left(\phi^{\rightarrow} {\color{#b880ff} +} \phi^{\leftarrow} \right) \qquad \text{and} \qquad \phi_m = \frac{1}{2}\left(\phi^{\rightarrow} {\color{#b880ff} -} \phi^{\leftarrow} \right)ϕe​=21​(ϕ→+ϕ←)andϕm​=21​(ϕ→−ϕ←)
